Red Attractor:
Exudes positive gravitational force - will attract asteroids.
Asteroids can fall into an attractor. A 'black hole'.

Blue Repeller:
Exudes negative gravitational force - repels asteroids.
Will split astreoids fallen into attractors. A 'white hole'.

Gray Boulder:
Obstacle. Asteroids will bounce off a boulder, losing velocity.

Asteroid:
The only moving entity. 
When collided with another asteroid, and if there's
a significant difference in sizes, the bigger asteroid 
will 'consume' the other one, growing in size.
